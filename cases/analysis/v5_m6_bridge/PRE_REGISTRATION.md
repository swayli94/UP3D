# GV5.0 PRE-REGISTRATION — M6 subsonic loose-coupling bridge (RECORDED)

Binding text: `docs/roadmap/track_v.md` **GV5.0** (added 2026-07-22,
user-directed): the 2.5-D → transonic-3-D bridge — the V3 loose driver on
the ONERA M6, giving the crossflow content (Ψ, B equations) its first live
3-D exercise before GV5.3. **Every metric is RECORDED, not pass/fail** (no
δ*(z) truth data exists for the M6). Committed before the first execution
(project discipline; the V3 precedent).

## Configuration (pinned)

- **Geometry/mesh**: ONERA M6 half wing, `cases/meshes/onera_m6/`
  `{coarse,medium}.msh` (gitignored, regenerated by `generate_onera_m6.py`;
  flat tip cap family), wake-cut by `mesh/wake_cut.py::cut_wake` at
  ingestion.
- **Flow condition**: M∞ = 0.5, α = 3.06° (the committed subsonic
  convention), γ = 1.4.
- **Viscous inputs**: forced transition x_tr/c = 0.05 on BOTH sides
  (project V3 convention; free transition is a recorded follow-up track
  item). Re_MAC = 11.72e6 (the M6 experiment reference value) ⇒
  `re_chord = 11.72e6 / 0.64607 = 1.81405e7` per meter (the meshes are in
  NASA meters; u_inf = ρ_inf = 1 ⇒ μ = 1/re_chord).
- **FP driver**: `solve/newton.py::solve_newton_lifting`, the production
  M6 recipe — `farfield_spanwise_gamma=True`, `precond="direct"`,
  `direct_refactor_every=1000`, `n_newton_max=60`,
  `kutta_estimator="pressure"` (P14), `tip_taper = vanish_smooth` with
  r_c = 0.05·b_semi (B32 production). Cold start (k = 0): probe-estimator
  solve first, pressure-estimator solve seeded from it with
  `n_picard_seed=0` (the P14 medium recipe — the quadratic Kutta row has
  the smaller basin). Outer iterations warm-start the pressure solve from
  the previous (φ, Γ). Transpiration enters via the V2 `external_rhs`
  channel (cut-mesh numbering).
- **IBL case** (`viscous/coupling.py::build_wing_case`, new):
  - INFLOW: LE band x/c ≤ 0.02 (local chord per section), Dirichlet-pinned
    to per-node laminar Blasius seeds, frozen at k = 1 (airfoil
    stagnation-band discipline).
  - OUTFLOW: natural at both TE lines (wake-cut TE duplication) and at the
    root section (z = 0 symmetry ⇔ natural zero-flux).
  - TIP MASK: z > 0.95·b_semi (the production tip_taper radius) is
    Dirichlet-pinned to per-node regime seeds AND masked out of the
    transpiration source (the GV3.3 tail-pin machinery; the tip-edge
    singularity zone is outside the VII validity envelope — track_v.md
    scope guards). Tip-band nodes appear in CSVs flagged `tip_masked` and
    are excluded from every statistic.
  - A4 u_e recovery zones: LE band x/c < 0.05 linear+smoothed (plus the
    tip band, robustness choice recorded here), elsewhere quadratic.
  - IBL numerics: eps_diff = 0.005, eps_diff_s = 0.02 (load-bearing V1
    calibration), ibl_tol 1e-9, max 100 Newton iterations per outer solve.
- **Loose loop**: ω = 1.0, ≤ 10 outer iterations, convergence =
  max|Δδ*|/max|δ*| < 1e-3 on raw successive IBL outputs (the GV3.2
  criterion). FP non-convergence and non-finite states hard-fail (GV3.3
  guardrails).

## Outputs (all RECORDED)

1. `gv5_0_history_{coarse,medium}.csv` — per-outer-iteration δ*max,
   δ* change, ṁ_max, IBL Newton stats, cl_p, cl_kj, cd_p.
2. **δ*(z) spanwise distributions** at local x/c ∈ {0.2, 0.4, 0.6, 0.8},
   upper/lower: `gv5_0_dstar_spanwise_{level}.csv` (z-binned mean/std of
   δ*/c_local, bins flagged inside the tip mask) +
   `gv5_0_dstar_spanwise_{level}.png`. *This is the feed for GV5.3's band
   pre-registration.*
3. **Crossflow field**: per-node surface dump
   `gv5_0_surface_{level}.csv` (x, y, z, x/c, side, δ*, H, B, Ψ, Cτ1, Cτ2,
   cf1) + `gv5_0_crossflow_{level}.png` (planform |B| and |Cτ2|/|Cτ1|
   fields + crossflow vs x/c); summary rows record max|B|/max|A|,
   max|Cτ2|/max|Cτ1|, max|Ψ| and WHERE the maxima sit (root/mid/tip).
4. **ΔCL**: cl_p and cl_kj at k = 0 (inviscid baseline) vs the coupled
   final iterate; direction recorded (expectation: DOWN — viscosity
   unloads; a smaller-than-2.5 % move is flagged input-limited per the A4
   discipline, medium mesh).
5. **Loop cost**: 3-D outer-iteration count vs the GV3.2 2.5-D count
   (4 coarse / 5 medium at the same criterion, ω = 1.0); IBL residual
   floor; per-level wall time.
6. `summary.csv` — every row RECORDED; the script exits 0 unless a hard
   guardrail fired (FP non-convergence / NaN), which raises instead.

## Registered expectations (not gates)

- ΔCL < 0 on both estimators (viscous unloading); magnitude honest-record.
- Crossflow nonzero but small inboard (swept-tapered wing at α 3.06:
  expect |B|/|A| ≪ 1 mid-span, larger toward root/tip junctions); the
  quasi-2-D lock is NOT expected to hold anywhere exactly — that is the
  point of the exercise.
- Loop convergence ≤ 10 outer iterations is the bridge question; a stall
  or runaway is recorded with its mechanism (the GV3.3 stern precedent:
  ṁ feedback gain > 1 shows as ṁ_max growth over k).
