Committed BEFORE the first execution, per discipline. Trigger: GV5.1
band (b) HONEST FAIL (`../v5_tight_coupling/VERDICT.md` §3) and its
consequence §5.1 — the augmented Newton's blocker is the intrinsic
floor of the steady IBL residual on the cond(J_BL,BL) ~ 4e10 near-null
manifold. This study is **diagnosis only**: no closure/formulation
edits, no solver changes, no Newton retries. Its output is the
evidence base for designing the regularization / projected-solve gate

question below is "answered" when its committed artifact exists and

## Questions

- **Q1 spectrum.** σ(J_BL,BL) at S1/S2/S3 (dense SVD, 1236²/2460² —
  exact at this scale). σmax, σmin, count below {1e-6, 1e-8, 1e-10}·σmax,
  spectral gaps. Does the near-null cluster persist at the converged
  state (S1/S2), or is it k=1-specific (S3)?
- **Q2 null-space anatomy.** The top ~20 smallest-σ right/left singular
  vectors at S1 (and S2 if the cluster persists): energy by state
  variable (δ, A, B, Ψ, Cτ1, Cτ2) and nodal support mapped to
  (x/c, side) — TE band? transition region? LE pin edge? CSV + PNG
  planform/strip maps.
- **Q3 scaling.** Row/column norm equilibration R·J·C: cond before/after,
  and the near-null count after scaling. How much of the 4e10 is a
  scaling artifact (the GV5.1 smoke saw raw dU O(5e2–6e3) in Λ/A/B
  rows) vs a true rank deficiency? Discriminates "solver-level fix
  (scaling)" from "formulation-level fix".
- **Q4 closure-floor activity.** Active set of the closure floors
  (DELTA_MIN and any other min/max clamps in `closures.py`) at S1/S2:
  node count and locations; overlap with the Q2 null support
  (floor-active rows have zero interior derivatives → candidate exact
  null directions).
- **Q5 residual anatomy at the floor.** F_BL at S1/S2 decomposed per
  (node, equation): where does the 1.7e-6/3.1e-6 live, which of the 6
  equations carries it, and its alignment with the Q2 left null vectors
  (|u_iᵀ F| distribution — is the residual nearly orthogonal to the
  range?).
- **Q6 formulation sensitivity probes (coarse, S1 only).** The F_BL
  floor level and near-null count under: (a) eps_diff ×0.5 and ×2;
  (b) the closure DELTA_MIN floor relaxed/tightened one notch (probe
  via the closures interface if exposed, otherwise via a logged local
  edit that is REVERTED in the same run, numbers recorded). One point
  each, recorded — discriminates "artificial-viscosity truncation
  floor" from "closure-floor floor".
- **Q7 pseudo-time stall anatomy (coarse, S1 only).** Standalone
  `IBL3Solver` pseudo-time from S1: per-iteration residual AND the
  controller's step size — does the step controller bottom out (a
  solver limitation) or does the residual plateau at full step (a
  formulation floor)? Recorded, feeds the GV5.1b design.
